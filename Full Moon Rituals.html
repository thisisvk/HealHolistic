Preparing for a Full Moon Ritual

Before diving into the rituals, preparation is key. A ritual is not about following rules it's about creating a sacred space for your energy, mind, and soul to align.

1. Set Your Intention

Decide what you wish to release and what you want to invite into your life. This could be stress, limiting beliefs, or negative patterns or new beginnings, peace, and abundance.

2. Create a Sacred Space

Choose a quiet place in your home or outdoors under the moonlight. Light candles, place crystals, and play soft healing music. If you cannot be outdoors, visualizing the moon's light can be just as effective.

3. Cleanse Your Energy

Using sage, incense, or even guided meditation for anxiety and stress relief, clear the energy of your space and your aura. Many participants at Heal Holistic's online reiki healing circles say that this cleansing sets the tone for deeper healing.
