What is Reiki Healing?

Reiki is a Japanese healing technique developed in the early 20th century by Mikao Usui. The word "Reiki" comes from Rei (universal) and Ki (life force energy).

The idea is simple yet profound: life force energy flows through every living being. When this energy is balanced, you feel physically healthy, emotionally stable, and spiritually connected. But when it's blocked or weakened, you may experience stress, illness, or emotional turbulence.

Reiki healing near me sessions typically involve a practitioner placing their hands lightly on or just above your body, channeling universal energy to restore balance. You don't need to "believe" in Reiki for it to work; the energy flows naturally to where it is needed most.
